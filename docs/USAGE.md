# Usage Guide

## Getting Started

After installation, SurfSafe automatically analyzes every page you visit.

---

## The Popup

Click the SurfSafe icon to see analysis results:

### Safety Gauge
- **Green (80-100)**: Safe
- **Yellow (50-79)**: Low/Medium risk
- **Red (0-49)**: High/Critical risk

### Risk Levels

| Level | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| SAFE | No threats detected |
| LOW | Minor concerns |
| MEDIUM | Potential issues |
| HIGH | Likely scam |
| CRITICAL | Confirmed threats |

## Tips

1. **Wait 2 seconds** - Analysis needs time
2. **Check confidence** - Higher = more reliable
3. **Read explanation** - Understand why
4. **Use whitelist** - Skip trusted sites
5. **Provide feedback** - Improves accuracy
